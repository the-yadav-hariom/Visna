A Legacy of Learning Excellence

Founded in 1985, RCH Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Siwan for over three decades. We are a co-educational institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), dedicated to fostering an environment where curiosity thrives and potential is unlocked. Our philosophy is rooted in a holistic approach to education, combining rigorous academics with a rich tapestry of extracurricular activities to build well-rounded individuals.

Our campus is designed to be a safe and inspiring space for students, featuring modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, a spacious library, and extensive sports facilities. We believe that learning extends beyond the textbook, and our dedicated educators are committed to nurturing critical thinking, creativity, and a strong sense of community.

We invite you to explore our school and see firsthand how we are preparing students to become compassionate, responsible, and successful global citizens.

From the Principal's Desk

Mr. Ashish Kumar

Welcome to RCH Public School, where we believe every child is a unique individual with immense potential. Our goal is to create a nurturing and challenging environment that inspires students to achieve academic excellence and personal growth. We are dedicated to providing a holistic education that not only focuses on core subjects but also on character building, critical thinking, and a sense of social responsibility.

Our committed team of educators works tirelessly to guide and support students on their educational journey. We encourage students to be curious, creative, and confident, preparing them to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

We invite you to join our school community and partner with us in shaping the future leaders of tomorrow. Thank you for entrusting us with your child's education.
